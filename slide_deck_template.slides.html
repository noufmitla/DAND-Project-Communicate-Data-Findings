by Nouf Alotaibi¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, we'll be taking a look at the bay Area bike share system users and their behaviour, the average trip duration, and the Monthly usage of the system.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset includes 1,851,950 trips with 14 feature:

  • Trip duration (sec)
  • Start time/End time
  • Start station id
  • Start station name
  • Start station latitude
  • Start station longitude
  • End station id
  • End station name
  • End station latitude
  • End station longitude
  • Bike id
  • User type (Customer/Subscriber)
  • Bike share for all trips (Yes/No)

Distribution of Ford GoBike System users¶

There are two types of users using the Ford GoBike System, subscribers who are members of the program, and customers, who are usually occasional riders or tourists. It was found that (subscriber) make 85% of all system customers, 15% of them were casual riders (Customer).

Average Trip Duration per Month¶

The point plot here shows the average trip durations per month. As we can see, the duration of the trips was consistent at the beginning of the year. June and July had the longest trips, while November and December had the shortest trips. It seems that the longer trips take place in the summer whereas trips were shorter in winter.

Monthly usage of Ford GoBike System per user type¶

Both subscribers and customers ride the most during the summertime, with subscribers spread out in October, and customers in July. Customers are likely tourists or casual riders, while Subscribers are most likely riders using the bikes for their daily routine. However, The usage was not very papular during the winter for both users.

Summary¶

Renting a bike from Ford GoBike System is an exciting way to get around the city. There are two types of users using the Ford GoBike System, subscribers who are members of the program, and customers, who are usually casual riders or tourists. Both subscribers and customers ride the most during the summertime, with subscribers spread out in October, and customers in July. Moreover, It looks that the longer trips take place in the summer whereas trips were shorter in winter.
